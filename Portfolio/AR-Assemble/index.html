Abstract

We presented an innovative application leveraging Augmented Reality (AR) technology to simplify the PC assembly process. With the growing demand for custom-built PCs in a booming market, assembling a computer remains a challenging task for many users. This app transforms traditional assembly guides into immersive AR simulations, providing users with an intuitive and interactive virtual assistant. Designed to empower non-professional users, it enables them to independently complete the complex task of building a PC with ease.
