Cosmos Echo

Kexiang Shuai, Jiaxin He, Jessica Li, Na Chen, Frank Lin

Abstract

Cosmos Echo is a 5-minute immersive VR narrative where players become a dying astronaut adrift in the silent void of space. With only five minutes of oxygen left, he uses a telescope to find celestial echoes of his family - Saturn's rings hum his wedding song, a comet recalls his daughter's kite. Between fading breath and endless stars, love becomes his final connection to the universe.

My role: Unity Programmer.
