Shopping Mini-Program in Wechat

Abstract

This project introduces a home service booking platform with a mobile app for users and a web-based system for merchants. The user-side app provides an intuitive experience for browsing, booking, and managing services like cleaning and appliance repair, with transparent pricing and streamlined workflows. The merchant-side platform offers tools for managing service listings, tracking orders, and customer communication through an efficient dashboard. By connecting users and service providers, the platform enhances convenience, reliability, and operational efficiency in the home service industry.

User-side Features (Mobile Platform)

This home service booking platform provides users with a highly intuitive and seamless mobile experience for scheduling household services. The interface offers a clean and straightforward layout, featuring distinct service categories such as daily cleaning, appliance repair, and installation. Users can browse through various service options and view detailed descriptions, pricing, and scope of each service. They can easily select the service duration, provide contact details, and specify preferred dates and times for appointments.

The platform is designed to ensure a smooth booking process, with a step-by-step form guiding users through essential fields such as phone numbers and names. Users are also presented with clear payment summaries before finalizing their orders. A “dynamic” section keeps users updated with articles related to home services, offering valuable tips and showcasing the platform's expertise. The design emphasizes transparency and user-friendliness, making it easy for individuals to find and book reliable services tailored to their needs.

Merchant-side Features (Web Platform)

The merchant interface, accessible via a web platform, serves as a comprehensive management tool for service providers. Merchants can oversee a wide range of operations, from managing service categories and listings to tracking orders. The dashboard provides clear and organized views of service details, including names, categories, pricing, and detailed descriptions. Merchants can easily add, edit, or remove services, ensuring that offerings remain up-to-date and competitive.

The platform also includes features for managing contact information, allowing merchants to maintain professional communication with clients. Additionally, it supports importing and exporting data, enabling seamless integration with external tools. The categorization system helps merchants structure their services logically, making them more discoverable to users. Overall, the merchant-side platform is a powerful, user-centric solution for managing and optimizing service offerings, ensuring efficient operations and enhancing customer satisfaction.
